Modern Watergate Salad

Swap out whipped topping for fresh whipped cream and add Amarena cherries for a welcome sour note in this fluffy dessert.

  • Prep Time:
    10 mins
  • Chill Time:
    1hr
  • Total Time:
    1 hr 10 mins
  • Servings
    8
Jump to Nutritious Fact

Ingredients

1X 2X 4X

  • 1 (8-ounce) can crushed pineapple in juice, undrained
  • 1 (3.4-ounce) package pistachio-flavor instant pudding and pie filling mix
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows
  • 1/2 cup chopped dry-roasted pistachio nuts, plus more for garnish
  • 1/2 cup whipping cream
  • 2 tablespoons powdered sugar
  • Amarena cherries in syrup, drained, for garnish

Step 1

Mix together pineapple, pudding mix, marshmallows, and pistachios in a large bowl. Beat cream and sugar in a medium bowl with an electric mixer at high speed until stiff peaks form. Fold whipped cream into pineapple mixture. Chill, covered, at least 1 to 2 hours or up to 1 day. Garnish with pistachios and cherries.
